The Best Gaming Laptops for 2020

Deb Cherryhill

The chill of the winter season is fast approaching, the bane of existence. Goodbye hiking and camping, and hello wintery blues. But, as avid gamers, we can take advantage of these colder months to, well, game! As Mary Poppins once said, A spoonful of sugar helps the medicine go down. To combat the winter blues, right now is the perfect time to prepare to hunker down into your tech bunker for a few months of intense gaming. In this article, we’ve compiled a comprehensive list of laptops that can help support high-intensity gaming with a low-intensity impact on your pocketbook.

#1: Dial Gamer 5000

Summary: A sleek new entry into this company’s lineup. It is 15% slimmer and lighter than the previous entry and packs a powerful punch with its improved RAM capacity and killer graphics card. It’s definitely on the pricier side, but it’ll surely last for a long time. Besides, you’ll probably get your money’s worth within the first few months given all the gaming you do.
Pros: Decent memory, great graphics card, excellent overall performance
Cons: Short battery life, hefty price tag
Verdict: Go ahead and buy it! You won’t regret it.
